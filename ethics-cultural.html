Cultural Adaptation Reflection

The integration process of me into the American engineering work environment has been a change and an eye-opener. As an Indian, I went through a moderate cultural shock as the American approach was somewhat different, coming to a country where the professional environment is more democratic and where deference to authority is inculcated in everyday life.
